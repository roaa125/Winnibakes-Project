Lahme B Ajine "Ground Beef" Mana'eesh "FlatBread"
Lahme b Ajine mana'eesh is a popular Middle Eastern flatbread topped with a flavorful mixture of finely ground meat, typically beef or lamb, combined with tomatoes, onions, parsley, and a blend of aromatic spices. The topping is spread thinly over soft dough and baked until the edges are lightly crisp and golden.
Akkawi Cheese Mana'eesh "FlatBread"
Akkawi Cheese Mana'eesh is a traditional Middle Eastern flatbread topped with generous amounts of Akkawi cheese. Finished with a sprinkle of black seeds, it offers a delicious combination of creamy cheese, soft bread, and subtle earthy flavor.
Beef Shawarma Pie in a Pan
Spiced beef strips are layered into soft dough and topped with tomatoes, parsley, onions, pickles, and creamy tahini. Baked until golden brown, it delivers a satisfying balance of savory, fresh, and tangy flavors.
Mediterranean Dippings
A selection of traditional Middle Eastern dips including hummus, labneh, mutabal, and a fresh vegetables plate. Served with warm pita bread, these appetizers provide rich flavors and refreshing textures.
Shakshouka
A rich homemade tomato sauce topped with two sunny-side-up eggs and seasoned with traditional herbs and spices. Served hot with fresh bread for dipping.
Sujuk & Cheese Mana'eesh
Flavorful beef sausage seasoned with traditional spices and topped with a generous layer of melted cheese. A hearty combination of bold flavors and comforting textures.
