Lahme B Ajine "Ground Beef" Mana'eesh "FlatBread"
Lahme b Ajine mana'eesh is a popular Middle Eastern flatbread topped with a flavorful mixture of finely ground meat, typically beef or lamb, combined with tomatoes, onions, parsley, and a blend of aromatic spices. The topping is spread thinly over soft dough and baked until the edges are lightly crisp and golden. The result is a savory, fragrant dish with a balance of rich meat flavors, fresh vegetables, and warm seasonings. Often enjoyed as a quick meal, snack, or breakfast, Lahme b Ajine is commonly served with fresh lemon wedges, vegetables, or yogurt, making it both satisfying and versatile.
Akkawi Cheese Mana'eesh "FlatBread"
Akkawi Cheese Mana'eesh is a traditional Middle Eastern flatbread topped with generous amounts of Akkawi cheese, a mild and slightly salty white cheese known for its smooth texture and excellent melting qualities. Baked until the dough is golden and the cheese is warm and bubbly, it is finished with a sprinkle of black seeds (nigella seeds) on top, adding a subtle earthy flavor and a delicate crunch. The combination of soft, fluffy bread, creamy melted cheese, and aromatic black seeds creates a simple yet satisfying dish that is enjoyed for breakfast, lunch, or as a light snack throughout the Middle East.
Beef Shawarma Pie in a Pan
The Beef Shawarma Pie in a Pan is a hearty and flavorful dish that brings together the bold taste of Middle Eastern shawarma and the comfort of freshly baked bread. Spiced beef strips are layered into soft dough and topped with tomatoes, parsley, onions, pickles, and creamy tahini, creating a rich combination of savory, fresh, and tangy flavors. Baked in a round pan until golden brown, the crust becomes lightly crisp on the outside while remaining soft and fluffy inside. Every bite delivers a satisfying balance of seasoned beef, vibrant vegetables, and smooth tahini, making it a delicious meal for sharing or enjoying on its own.
Mediteranean Dippings
Our selection of traditional Middle Eastern dips includes creamy hummus made from blended chickpeas, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic; refreshing labneh, a thick strained yogurt drizzled with olive oil; and smoky mutabal, crafted from roasted eggplant, tahini, lemon juice, and garlic. Served alongside a fresh vegetables plate featuring a variety of crisp seasonal vegetables such as cucumbers, tomatoes, lettuce, and radishes, these appetizers offer a delicious combination of rich flavors, smooth textures, and refreshing freshness that pairs perfectly with warm pita bread and any meal.
Shakshouka
Shakshouka is a comforting and flavorful Middle Eastern breakfast dish featuring a rich homemade tomato sauce seasoned with herbs and spices, topped with two perfectly cooked sunny-side-up eggs. The eggs are gently cooked in the sauce, allowing their creamy yolks to blend with the savory tomato base. Served hot with fresh bread for dipping, Shakshouka offers a delicious balance of hearty flavors and satisfying textures, making it a popular choice for breakfast, brunch, or any time of day.
Sujuk "Sausauge" and Cheese Mana'eesh "Flatbread"
Sujuk & Cheese is a savory and satisfying dish featuring flavorful beef sausage seasoned with traditional spices and topped with a generous layer of melted cheese. The rich, slightly spicy taste of the sujuk pairs perfectly with the creamy, golden cheese, creating a delicious combination of bold flavors and comforting textures. Served hot and freshly baked, it is a popular choice for breakfast, lunch, or a hearty snack.
